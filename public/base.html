Choose your own Adventure

For Your Retreats

Veda I and Veda II are ideal for yoga, meditation or any other kind of wellness retreats. You can offer your customer your vision and your product with our healthy food, detox programs, sacred excursion and any kind of massage. You focus on having the best in class product. We take care of the rest.

For Your Business Off-site

You want to organize an off-site for your company. Do you look for a perfect place to bring your leaders together and build the foundations of an aware organization with healthy food and wellness practices and programs? Veda I and Veda II provides you the perfect concept to inspire your leaders to become the conscious leaders of tomorrow.

Your Remote Workspace

You want to enjoy time away from home while working on your projects. This is the ultimate experience for you if you need a quiet place where you can organise your thoughts while running your business in a private escape.

For your Family and Friends Holidays

The perfect concept to make your family gathering a unique happening. The different high energy places on the Nile combined with healthy, honest and organic food and with real care for the body connects you back to your inner core. From this deep feeling, you make a beautiful connection with your loved ones and magic happens.

Kristof

Contact with Isabelle

Isabelle

After finishing law school and an MBA, she created her own firm for interim legal solutions, until a progressed form of lyme disease called her to a halt. Her strong and never ending will to regain her full health drove her to acquire deep knowledge of the body, emotions, the mind and the soul. True healing only came when she combined the bodily work with emotional and spiritual healing and understanding of the Self. Healing is stepping into your own power and getting to know the true self. Helping to grow people in awareness and step back in their own power is a true passion of Isabelle.

Contact with Amparo

Amparo

Amparo loves inspiring others in their research of a healthiest lifestyle and happiness
