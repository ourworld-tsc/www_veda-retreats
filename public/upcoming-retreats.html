Release what no longer serves you
14 - 19 August 2017 (6 days / 5 nights)

Reawaken your senses

Reawaken your senses through detoxing and rebooting your body, mind, heart, spirit, and soul. Feel more balanced and aware by joining us on this journey and following our unique formula based on personal approach of one on one consultations with our international therapists, healers and professionals as well as guided group activities, in a beautiful setting with rich history and organic environment known as and voted the Greenest place in Egypt - El Gouna.

Start a journey

We often get into a pattern of "overdoing it" on a physical, mental, emotional levels without realizing it. You will be offered an opportunity and will be encouraged to reboot your system and start a journey towards a balanced way of being. You will be offered tools to help you sustain high energy levels and a peaceful mind regardless of your external environment.

This retreat is for

Those who are ready to let go off old patterns and habits.
Those who are ready to fully commit and make positive changes in their lives.
Those who are ready to follow the guidance of our team of experts, gain knowledge and take consistent action towards discovering and sustaining their perfect state of balance and becoming more aware!
